# Example — Research Productivity Tool

## Concept
Literature triage assistant for applied science labs.

## Idea stage output
- Problem: researchers spend hours filtering irrelevant papers.
- ICP: small biotech and university labs.
- Test: 12 pilot users; measure time saved in weekly review.

## MVP scope
- Topic ingestion
- Paper relevance scoring
- Structured summary export

## Launch controls
- Citation traceability
- Confidence flags
- Human verification requirement before downstream decisions

## Scale moves
- Team knowledge graph
- Experiment-to-literature linking
- Organization-level workflow automation
