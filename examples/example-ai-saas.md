# Example — AI SaaS Product

## Concept
AI meeting intelligence tool for B2B sales teams.

## Idea stage output
- Problem: reps lose deal context after calls.
- ICP: SMB sales orgs with 5–50 reps.
- Test: 15 interviews; success if 8+ confirm painful manual recap workflow.

## MVP scope
- Call transcript import
- Auto summary + action items
- CRM note sync (one platform)

## Launch metrics
- Weekly active teams
- Summary-to-CRM sync rate
- 30-day retention

## Scale moves
- Multi-CRM support
- Admin analytics
- Compliance controls by segment
