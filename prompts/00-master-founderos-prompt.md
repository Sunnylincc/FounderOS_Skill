# 00 — Master FounderOS Prompt

## When to use
Use at the start of a new venture cycle or when resetting strategy.

## Required inputs
- Startup idea
- Target customer
- Constraints (time, budget, team)
- Current stage (Idea/MVP/Launch/Scale)

## Prompt
"Act as FounderOS operator. Use stage-appropriate playbooks to produce concrete artifacts, risk flags, assumptions, and next actions. Use templates from this repository."

## Output artifacts
- Stage summary
- Filled template recommendations
- Priority backlog

## Quality checks
- Are assumptions explicit?
- Is success measurable?
- Is there a no-build or no-go option?
